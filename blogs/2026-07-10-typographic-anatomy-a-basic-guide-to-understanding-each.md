A Basic Guide to Understanding Each Part of a Letter Typography is present in almost everything we see: books, websites, apps, posters, packaging, brand identities, and social media. However, even though we’re constantly surrounded by letters, we rarely stop to observe how they’re constructed. Understanding the structure of a letter may seem like a topic reserved for specialized designers, but it’s actually useful knowledge for anyone who works with visual communication.
