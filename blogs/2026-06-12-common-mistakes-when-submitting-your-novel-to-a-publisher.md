Publishing Begins Before You Submit Your Manuscript Finishing a novel is one of the greatest achievements for any writer. After months (or even years) of work, the time comes to consider the next step: finding a publisher willing to take a chance on the project. However, many authors discover that writing a good story isn’t always enough.
