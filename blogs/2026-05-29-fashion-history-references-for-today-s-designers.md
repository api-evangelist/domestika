Knowing the history of fashion is vital to have logical references . Looking at fashion history need not be a nostalgic exercise or a constant search for "vintage". For many contemporary designers, studying the past functions more as a creative tool: a way to understand how certain forms, materials and symbols continue to evolve over time.
