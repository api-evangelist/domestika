What changes when you turn writing into a habit Some people imagine writing as something that happens only at certain moments: when a great idea appears, when inspiration strikes, or when there is enough time and concentration. But many times writing is not strengthened by waiting for the perfect moment, but by repeating a small gesture constantly. Writing every day may seem like a simple, even unspectacular habit.
