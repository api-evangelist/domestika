Challenges the idea that creativity requires sudden inspiration, arguing instead that inspiration is built through habits, observation, curiosity, and practice. Offers practical strategies such as observing daily life closely, developing systems for capturing ideas, exploring disciplines outside one's specialty, and establishing small creative rituals, while warning against waiting for perfect ideas or over-consuming references.
