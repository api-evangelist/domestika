Defines one-point perspective using the horizon line, vanishing point, and converging lines, then presents three progressive exercises: drawing a cube in perspective, transforming the cube into furniture, and constructing a room or hallway. Covers common mistakes such as lines failing to converge, attempting complex scenes too early, and ignoring the horizon line.
