Learn how to protect, center, and frame your artwork without using adhesive materials that could damage the paper The timeless look of an ink drawing can become even more striking when it is displayed in the right frame. Whether you have created an architectural illustration, a botanical sketch, or a detailed artwork with fine liners, framing allows you to protect your work and give it a professional finish. However, attaching an original drawing directly to a backing board with tape or glue can damage the paper over time.
